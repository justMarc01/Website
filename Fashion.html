Fashion Facilities

Show rooms

Showrooms are spaces where fashion brands or designers display their collections to buyers, press, and industry professionals. These facilities are designed to create a visually appealing environment that highlights the clothing and accessories. Showrooms often have fitting rooms, display racks, and appropriate lighting to showcase the garments effectively.

Garment Production Facilities

These facilities include factories or workshops equipped with specialized equipment and machinery for garment production. They have cutting tables, sewing machines, pressing stations, and quality control areas to facilitate the efficient and precise manufacturing of clothing.

Design Studios

Fashion design studios are creative workspaces where designers conceptualize and develop their collections. These studios are equipped with design tools, pattern-making equipment, sewing machines, and workstations. They provide a conducive environment for designers to sketch, drape, experiment with fabrics, and create prototypes.
