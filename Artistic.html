Art Facilities

Art studios

Art studios are dedicated spaces equipped with materials and tools specific to different art forms, such as painting, sculpture, ceramics, printmaking, or photography. They provide artists with a conducive environment to work, experiment, and develop their artistic practice.

Artist residencies

Artist residencies offer dedicated time and space for artists to live and work in a supportive community. These facilities often provide studio spaces, accommodation, and sometimes funding or stipends for artists to focus on their creative projects without distractions.

Performance Spaces

These facilities cater to performing arts, including theaters, concert halls, dance studios, and rehearsal spaces. They provide stages, sound systems,lighting setups, and seating arrangements to accommodate live performances, rehearsals, and workshops.
